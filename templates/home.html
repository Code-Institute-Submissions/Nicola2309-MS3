The Idea Behind Our Cookbook

This online recipe book found its purpose on the recently released Harvard Study on the perfect diet for humans and the planet, the "Planetary Health Diet".
Founded on years of studies on food cultures around the world it sums up the perfect proportion of Whole Grains, Vegetables and Healthy Proteins which generally guarantee enormous benefits on the human body nutrition and health; interestingly what is generally the best for a healthy body is specularly the best use for the planet soil and see resources.
These clear results provide us with a higher-quality and food-richer future through a simple tool, a plate!

If your Food Passion turns into "Nerdy" at times, here are some articles you'll sure enjoy:
  • Plate and the Planet:

    In this article is discussed the differnt impact of the different food products on the planet and the suggested quantity to eat (in grams per day) calculated on a healthy body in order to keep it so.

  • How to Shopping and Storing:

    This article contains shopping tips to minimize food waste, as well as tips to store and use the products when they are at their best.

  • Weekly Meal Prep:

    As The title says, here you'll find some ideas on how to build a week meal plan, the advantages of it, and the storing times for your cooked or cut food.

  • Strategies for Eating Well on a Budget:

    Even though the article is focused on the American Market the strategies outlined can be used by everyone who struggles with managing the fridge and the pocket in a healthy way.

  • Healthy Diets from Sustainable Food Systems:

    This is an extensive article from The Lancet with a big amount of data on the relations between healthy diets and resources management.

  • Food, livestock production, energy, climate change, and health:

    A long historical view till nowadays consumption, a specific section on the relation between agricultural and red meat emissions and prospects for food yields.
